Accomodation

Rathsallagh house has a total of 39 rooms available. We are required to fill a minimum of 24 rooms on the night of the wedding, so if you’d like to stay at the House and to join us for breakfast in the morning, we would be both delighted and grateful!

As further encouragement for you to stay we have subsidised rooms on the night of the wedding to a rate of €160. Room rates are based on two adults sharing, a supplement of €75 applies to an additional adult in a room. Children under 12yrs are €35pp. All rooms include breakfast.

Also on Rathsallgh site, they have two lodges available; Eagle Lodge €405 – Sleeps 6 in 3 en suite room, so €67.50 PPS B&B. Gate Lodge €375 - Sleeps 6 in 3 rooms, One en suite ( €145 ) , two share a bathroom ( €115 )

This will be at a first come, first served basis.

Things to do nearby

There are so many beautiful places to visit in Ireland. Here are some of our top recommendations;

Glendalough

Glendalough is renowned for it’s magnificent scenery but is also full of a rich and varied heritage in terms of history, monuments, archaeology, architecture, landscapes, geology, parks, flora, fauna, wildlife habitats & mining history. http://www.glendalough.ie

Powerscourt Waterfall

Powerscourt Waterfall is Ireland’s highest at 121m (398ft.) and is 6km from the Main Estate. It is set in one of Ireland’s most beautiful parklands at the foothills of the Wicklow Mountains. http://powerscourt.com/waterfall

Newgrange

Newgrange is a prehistoric monument in County Meath, Ireland. It was built during the Neolithic period, around 3200 BC, making it older than Stonehenge and the Egyptian pyramids. The site consists of a large circular mound with an inner stone passageway and chambers. http://www.newgrange.com

Tayto Park

Tayto Park is a crisp/chip themed amusement park in Ireland. It is located in the townland of Kilbrew, very near Ashbourne in County Meath. Located 30 minutes from Dublin, Tayto Park hosts a variety of activities suitable for all ages. http://www.taytopark.ie

Guinness Storehouse

Located in the heart of the St. James's Gate Brewery, the Guinness Storehouse® is Ireland's most popular tourist attraction. It's the home of the Black Stuff, the heart of Dublin and an unforgettable start to your Irish adventure. The journey begins at the bottom of the world's largest pint glass and continues up through seven floors filled with interactive experiences that fuse our long brewing heritage with Ireland's rich history. https://www.guinness-storehouse.com/en

Dublin Zoo

Dublin Zoo is much more than a fun-filled, stimulating day out for all the family... it’s a place to learn about wild animals, especially those which are endangered. Located in the Phoenix Park in the heart of Dublin city, Dublin Zoo is Ireland’s most popular family attraction, and welcomed over one million visitors last year. As one of the world’s oldest, yet popular zoos, the 28 hectare park in the heart of Dublin is home to some 400 animals in safe environment where education and conservation combine for an exciting and unforgettable experience! https://www.dublinzoo.ie
